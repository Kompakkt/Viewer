Show octree boxes

Toggle the visibility of boxes displaying the loading zones of the octree.

Reaching a loading zone with the camera will initiate loading of the respective octree of the point cloud, if the octree is in the configured level of detail.

A larger box indicates a lower level of detail, and smaller boxes indicate higher levels of detail.
